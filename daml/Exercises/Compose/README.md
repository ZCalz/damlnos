## Preview

Real applications compose multiple templates and choices into larger workflows.

### What you will learn

- Role templates — a party's standing invitation to participate
- Delegation — letting another party act on your behalf via a choice
- Linking contracts: a choice that fetches, exercises, and creates in one step
- `fetch` inside choices — read another contract's state mid-transaction
- Atomic multi-step transactions
